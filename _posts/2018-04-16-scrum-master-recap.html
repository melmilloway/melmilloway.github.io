This past Spring xAPI Cohort, Ross Kerr and I teamed up to create a project in Storyline 360 with animated video.

One of my major goals for the project, was to bring data back into Storyline from the LRS. We also wanted to test to see if how users interact with the video impacted their score in the game. Lastly, we wanted to test engagement and UX throughout the trivia.

We chose a common and somewhat dry topic for our project. We chose Scrum as a topic because it is a common topic and there is not too much engaging learning out there on it.

Our plan was to create a world in which users would learn about Scrum. Different forms of the trivia would be released on a regular basis as a part of a larger more blended project on scrum

In this post, I won't cover our process or findings but rather allow you to check out what we made.
